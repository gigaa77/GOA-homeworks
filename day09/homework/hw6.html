FOOD


Food is any substance consumed to provide nutrients and energy for living organisms. It usually contains:

  1. Carbohydrates → main source of energy (e.g. rice, bread, fruit)
  2. Proteins → build and repair tissues (e.g. meat, beans, eggs)
  3. Fats → long-term energy storage and cell function (e.g. oils, nuts, dairy)
