BMW Motorrad

BMW Motorrad is the motorcycle division of BMW, known for producing high-quality, technologically advanced, and reliable motorcycles.

Founded in 1923, the brand has a long history of engineering' innovation—starting with the legendary BMW R32, its first motorcycle with a boxer engine.

    1. Boxer Engine Heritage:

BMW is famous for its flat-twin boxer engines, used in many touring and adventure models like:

  • R 1250 GS
  • R nineT series
  • R 1250 RT


During the 1930s the company grew rapidly, improving engine reliability and achieving notable racing success

including innovations like the R12, which was among the first motorcycles to use hydraulically damped telescopic forks
