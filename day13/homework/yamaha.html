YAMAHA

    1. Company Background / History:

Yamaha Motor Co., Ltd. is a Japanese company, founded on July 1, 1955. It was spun off from Nippon Gakki (now Yamaha Corporation, which makes musical instruments).

Founder / first president: Genichi Kawakami. Headquarters: Iwata, Shizuoka, Japan. As of recent data, Yamaha Motor has tens of thousands of employees and hundreds of subsidiaries.

Because Yamaha produces many types of motorcycles, its model lineup can be very complex, making it sometimes confusing for buyers.

Maintaining high performance + reliability while innovating (e.g., concept bikes) is always a balance.
