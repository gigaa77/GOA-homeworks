bmw

BMW — (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG) is a German luxury automobile and motorcycle manufacturer, known worldwide for its performance, precision engineering, and elegant design.

Key Facts:

  • Founded: 1916, Munich, Germany
  • Full Name: Bayerische Motoren Werke (Bavarian Motor Works)
  • Headquarters: Munich, Germany
  • Slogan: “The Ultimate Driving Machine”
